Tech Tips For a Better Digital Life

In today's digital age, our personal data is often collected and sold by large corporations without our explicit consent. As we navigate the online world, our privacy is frequently compromised leaving us vulnerable to data breaches and targeted advertising. The importance of open-source technology cannot be overstated as it offers greater transparency and control. This page is dedicated to exploring the critical aspects of digital privacy and provides practical tips on how to safeguard your online presence. Additionally, I'll be listing various open-source apps and alternatives to native/stock options (mainly for Android) that offer enhanced security and privacy empowering you to take control of your digital footprint.
